Welcome to Astronomy Club

Welcome to the Ithaca College Astronomy Club! If you’re fascinated by stars, galaxies, and the mysteries of the universe, you’ve come to the right place. Our club is a community for space enthusiasts to explore the cosmos together. We host stargazing events, workshops, and engaging discussions about all things astronomy. Whether you’re a seasoned stargazer or just beginning your journey into the universe, there’s something for everyone. Join us and be part of exciting adventures that bring the wonders of the night sky closer than ever!
